I have made my payment but I did not receive my clicks, what should I do?+

Simply visit the Purchase History section to view the Status. You'll find a Progress Bar there, indicating the status of lead delivery. Typically, we initiate click deliveries within 7 calendar days of purchase. For instance, if you make a purchase on January 1st, 2024, deliveries will commence by January 7th, 2024.

Can I refund my order? +

Unfortunately, refunds aren't available for Everleads. We are committed to delivering ONLY top-quality leads. Each click is a valuable investment, and every subscriber contributes to your assets.

I lost my invoice, how do I get it back? +

Simply click on Purchase History on the left panel.

Can I purchase leads in advance? +

Yes you can. You may purchase it in advance and indicate when you would like your start date to be! Click on this button to start.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept payments through Paypal for now.

The leads delivery is too slow, can I speed it up?+

In simple terms, after you make a purchase, we start sending clicks to your Destination URL within 7 calendar days. Then, each day after that, you'll receive 250 clicks until you reach your total. For example, if you buy clicks on July 1st, you'll start getting clicks by July 7th, and you'll continue to get 250 clicks every day. By July 10th, you'll have received all 1,000 clicks.

I want to change my Destination URL, is that possible?+

Once we've begun sending clicks to your chosen destination URL, you won't be able to alter it. However, before you make a purchase, you have the flexibility to divide the clicks you've purchased among multiple URLs for split testing purposes. For example, if you purchase 3,000 clicks, you can allocate 1,000 clicks to each of three different URLs of your choosing.

Targeted leads are leads who are already interested in your offer! To simply put, they belong to the niche you are targeting. That means they are more likely to purchase your product! Everleads are Targeted Leads!
